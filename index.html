Sydny is a Nigerian alternative hip-hop artiste and indie music producer. He combines hip-hop and folk elements in his music, with a baritone-voiced poetic lyricism. His unique style and expression appeals to an audience of young millennials who can relate to the regular struggles of his not-so-regular life.
